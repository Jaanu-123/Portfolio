About the Artist

Mandapalli Jahnavi

Passionate artist || Blending technical skill with artistic creativity

A Little About Me: I paint to capture what words often can’t — feelings, thoughts, and the quiet beauty of everyday life. Through Artistic Splashes, I explore different art forms that connect imagination with emotion. My work reflects both creativity and balance, shaped by an engineer’s precision and an artist’s heart.

Career Highlights

  • Fevicryl Certified Professional: Certified to conduct art-related workshops.
  • Head of Creative Crew: Led creative team for events at Central University of Karnataka.
  • Head of Art Department (Techkriti): Managed art department and event branding.
  • Gold Medal Winner: Awarded for Concept Art at Konaseema Chitra Kala Parishad.
